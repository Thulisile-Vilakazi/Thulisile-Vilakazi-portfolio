About

I’m a Software Engineering student at WeThinkCode_ with a background in law and a growing focus on data engineering and backend development. My work blends analytical thinking from legal studies with technical problem-solving. I build data pipelines, backend APIs, and systems that are maintainable and well-structured.

Through hands-on, project-based learning at WeThinkCode_, I’ve gained experience in Python, Java, SQL, and Linux/Unix systems. I use Agile methodologies, Test-Driven Development (TDD), and version control to deliver reliable, production-ready code in collaborative teams.

I’m passionate about using technology to make processes more accessible and transparent. Whether it’s automating transport schedules, simplifying legal information, or analysing data for security insights, I aim to build tools that make a real difference.

My Approach

I combine structured thinking with creativity to solve problems thoughtfully and efficiently. I write clean, maintainable code, test often, and focus on building solutions that are both reliable and easy to evolve. For me, good software is about clarity, purpose, and impact.

How I Collaborate

I believe great work happens in teams that communicate openly and learn from one another. I value clear documentation, meaningful feedback, and a shared sense of ownership. I’m proactive, curious, and enjoy helping projects move from idea to execution.

What I’m Looking For

An opportunity to contribute to meaningful projects where I can grow as an engineer, learn from others, and bring fresh energy to the team. I’m adaptable, eager to learn new technologies, and motivated by solving real problems that make a difference.
